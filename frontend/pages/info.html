THE PROBLEM

Large public events create complex waste management challenges.

01

Waste Generation

Festivals, pilgrimages and large gatherings can generate significant amounts of plastic, food, flower and other solid waste.

02

Pollution Risk

Waste accumulation and environmental conditions can increase pollution risks around rivers, roads and public spaces.

03

Workforce Planning

Cleanup teams need better estimates of workforce demand before and during high-crowd events.

MACHINE LEARNING SYSTEM

Three prediction stages power Aagaaz.

REGRESSION

Waste Prediction Model

Estimates total waste generation using event, location, crowd and environmental conditions.

Output: Total Waste Tons
CLASSIFICATION

Pollution Risk Model

Uses event conditions and predicted waste to estimate the expected pollution risk category.

Output: Pollution Risk Level
REGRESSION

Workforce Prediction Model

Estimates the number of cleanup workers required for the predicted event conditions.

Output: Workers Required
